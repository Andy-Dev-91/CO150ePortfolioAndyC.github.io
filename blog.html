Unit 2 Reflection

October 21, 2025

Over the course of mod 2 I have been learning how to do thorough research and produce a research report with in text citations. I have found it pretty difficult to find sources that are against my opinion of how artificial intelligence is making user experiences worse. I feel like I will need to change my topic. The most important thing that I learned through my research is that many others online share my opinion that AI being added to user interfaces is not always a good thing. Sometimes they are helpful when end users know how to use them, but more often than not they just get in the way, or want to sell you their own version of chatGPT. Microsoft does something like this with their AI tool ‘copilot’ and I constantly think to myself, “Why do I need something like this in my word processor, email client, and other tools that I use on a daily basis?” Although I have not tried these features in my office software, I think that they are unnecessary. I think I should find someone that does use the copilot features in MS office and see how they use it. Does it bring better understanding or just make some tasks more simple? During my research I saw many arguments that were stating that they did not appreciate all the extra AI features that have been added to operating systems and software. I want to find some resources that point out how helpful they are, how they increase productivity and take care of the menial tasks that most people do not want to engage with. Unfortunately, due to my bias I do not believe that my thinking has evolved from when I first started this topic. But there is still time for me to continue to perform great research and change my mind/stance. I am looking forward to continuing to do my research and learn how different people interact with computers and how AI helps or hinders them while using their computers. From the peer workshop that was online, I feel like I need to find better sources and improve on my research processes. The feedback that I received from my classmate was VERY helpful and helped me realize my bias. When I work on the next part of this project I will need to to keep my bias in check and find viewpoints that counter what I think and feel. When looking for more articles I think my new starting point will be how great UI and tools added to software can make us feel more connected and more productive. One specific goal for improving my writing in the next module is becoming better at citations. I want to do this so that I can carry on this skill to my future classes. It’s very important to have proper citations so that I do not get into trouble for academic dishonesty or plagiarism and receive a zero or negative grade on an assignment.
